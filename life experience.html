HELLO! This is a little insight to myself as a person. Aspects of being "Alejandro Lijo" include being an uncle, a brother, a student, a worker, and a son. I am a very outgoing person whenever I am comfortable with someone. I am always willing to help which I think is an important quality that all people should have because everyone needs a little bit of help here and there. My entire family is from Spain and I was the only one that was actually born in the United States. Like most immigrant families, my parents came here for a better life and have been working hard ever since, which is where I get my work ethic from. I tend to remain calm in time that I need to remain calm. This is definitely due to the jobs that I have had over the course of the last couple of years. Since I was 16, I have worked at a funeral home as the doorman and just a general worker there. Whenever my boss, Mark, needs me, I go in and help with whatever I can, whether that be technical issues, translating for spanish funerals, or shoveling snow the night of a wake. Aside from that, I have also worked over the summer at a cemetery cutting grass, at a medical billing company called PGM, and at a trucking company called NEMF in the overnight billing department. I have been working since I was 16, and haven't had a summer off since. But all fun and no play makes Alejandro a tired zombie. So I have hobbies and interests such as traveling and kayaking that I absolutely enjoy.
